Our Services

Welcome to Extreme Concrete Makeover We enhance the look of your outdoor leisure areas by renewing and refining existing pool decks, patios, driveways, and garage floors, adding a touch of style and sophistication. Have pavers? We offer sealing services for those as well!

Restoration & Resurfacing

Restoration and resurfacing play essential roles across diverse fields, spanning construction, architecture, and the preservation of historical artifacts. These processes are centered on rejuvenating or improving the functionality, appearance, or structural strength of surfaces or objects. Whether applied to a building's facade, a roadway, or a work of art, restoration and resurfacing are pivotal in the ongoing efforts to sustain and rejuvenate structures and materials.

Decorative Concrete Decking

Decorative concrete decking represents a versatile and aesthetically pleasing approach to outdoor flooring and design. Concrete, once primarily associated with utilitarian purposes, has evolved into a material that can be transformed into visually appealing and functional surfaces for decks, patios, and outdoor living spaces.
